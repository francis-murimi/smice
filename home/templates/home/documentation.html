User Data
Accounts

This site requires the users to have an account to have full access to our services. Only username, first name, last name and email details are needed.These are for contacting you when necessary.

Order Details

These are details that will be used for delivery planning and making payments. These details are:

  1. Full name of the person the products will be delivered to.
  2. Location of where the bought products will be delivered to.
  3. Phone number. This is the phone number that will be used to make payments and to contact the buyer during the delivery process.
Any order made in one profile can have different order details

These details can be updated but are only updated if the order has not been payed for. To update, go to profile/orders.

Payments
Making payment

The payment method is Mpesa. You can pay for any order at any time. The amount to pay is automatically sent to your Mpesa application and the phone number used is that which you provided for order details. If the total amount to be paid is more than you Mpesa balance, Mpesa will inform you. Only Mpesa knows your balance. We do not know. If this will be the case, you can top up and make the payment later. If not the case, you will be prompted to complete the transaction.

Initializing the process

To make a payment, click the Pending payment link in the order you want to make payment for. You can pay for an order only once. After making the payment, confirm the payment and you will get the response.

Delivery

Delivery will be only be done for payed orders. The delivery period will vary with the product you ordered (indicated in the product details). If the given time is exceeded, contact us for more information.
